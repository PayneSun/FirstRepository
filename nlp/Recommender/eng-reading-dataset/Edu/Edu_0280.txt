My teacher is special…and sexy
Meet Zhu Songhua, dubbed by Internet users as "China’s Sexiest Female Teacher." The 30-year-old language teacher in Natong, Jiangsu Province gained popularity originally in early September after appearing on Jiangsu Satellite Television’s Happy Night program’s "My Teacher Is Special" segment where she caused a stir, with the audience immediately praising her beauty.When Zhu was asked about teaching, she replied that she believes there are no bad students, only bad teachers, that educating students about love and responsibility is the most important thing, and when she encounters a naughty child, she doesn’t severely criticize but rather patiently guides them.She’s also smart. Zhu won first place in the school’s young teachers teaching competition, won second prize in the Nantong Chongchuan district teaching competition and first prize for the Nantong Chongchuan district chalk writing competition. She has also written many articles published in the province’s magazine.Despite the slew of sexy photos she’s posted, Zhu said, "I’m just an ordinary person who just wants to live a normal life. I’m not trying to gain publicity."
